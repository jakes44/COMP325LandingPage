How it Works

Gain Goggles is an application for helping weightlifters to improve their form. Simply point your camera at the person exercising, and get tips on how to improve form through realtime analysis.

We use machine learning to model your (the weightlifter's) body position and determine movement. With this, we can see where you go wrong, even providing a 3D model of your body motion showing how you move and how you can get better.
